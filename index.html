About

Hi there! I am a rising senior at NYU Abu Dhabi majoring in Music and minoring in Interactive Media. I love making music and creating projects that implement sound in creative and compelling ways. In this website you can explore some of my projects I have done at NYU Abu Dhabi and outside university.
Apart from music, I have an interest in visual communication and developed a small personal project called Blue Sandwich Bleu. In this project one of my main activities was sharing Costa Rican music bands producing simple infographics and rating their best songs. This project is on pause, but will resume soon to keep improving my design skills.
